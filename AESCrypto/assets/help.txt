AES Crypto encrypts text messages. When message is encrypted it is automatically copied to clipboard.

To decrypt: copy encrypted message in another app, open AES Crypto and enter password.

AES Crypto uses AES encryption with a 256-bit key. New salt and IV is generated for each encryption.
